Philips Avent - uGrow

Android Developer - Team Lead

With uGrow, Philips Avent tries to support young parents in bringing up their baby. By means of an iOS and Android app parents can track all sorts of moments of their infant, ranging from how long their baby slept, drank or their temperature. Various devices can be connected to the app, such as bluetooth thermometers. uGrow is trying to target not only leasure, but as a medical grade product, also target prevention and professional use.

As the techlead of the Android development team I am involved with the development of the Android app from day one. I am responsible for the major app architecture, and gatekeeper of code quality (with an TICS A score). Close collaboration with the Backend and iOS teams makes sure that the experience of the app feels prestine across platforms, and together with the design team we make sure that both Philips Avent branding and platform conventions are respected.

Philips Lighting - Hue Point of Sales

Android Developer

Philips Hue is a connected light bulb system that let's the user control the color, intensity and temperature of their lights from their phone. It has an open SDK, so any developer could create their own Hue app.

For stores acros Europe, Philips Lighting needed a dedicated shop shop demo app, both for Android and iOS. Customers can play with the demo lights in the shop, so the app must be extremly robust. Also the app features large video content, localized to the users' preference, without network connection.

Released in the Play Store.
